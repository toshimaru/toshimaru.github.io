Why this color?

I used white and dark blue and green as a base color because in the given image, green was used as a button color and dark blue is used in the section of "additional infomation".

I reversed color. I used dark blue in the price area and white in the additional infomation. It is because I want to use whited letters in the area I want to emphasize. In this case, I thought I should empasize the price. That's why I use whited letters and dark blued backgroud.
